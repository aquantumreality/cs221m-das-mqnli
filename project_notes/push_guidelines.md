# Push Guidelines

Push:
- README.md
- CLAUDE.md
- requirements*.txt
- src/**/*.py
- scripts/*.py
- scripts/*.sh
- configs/*.yaml
- notebooks/*.ipynb
- notebooks/reference/*.ipynb
- project_notes/*.md
- small outputs/results/*.csv
- small outputs/figures/*.png

Do not push:
- .venv/
- __pycache__/
- .ipynb_checkpoints/
- outputs/models/
- outputs/logs/
- checkpoints/
- wandb/
- runs/
- *.pt
- *.pth
- *.bin
- *.safetensors
- tutorial_data/
- paper PDFs unless deliberately needed
